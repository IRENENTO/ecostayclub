Our Mission & Vision

Driving Sustainable Change from the Heart of Rwanda

Our Mission

To cultivate Green Havens across university campuses, empowering students to build climate resilience through hands-on conservation and indigenous reforestation.

Our Vision

A Rwanda where every student leads the creation of Green Havens, fostering a culture of climate resilience and environmental stewardship.
